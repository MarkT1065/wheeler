About Base Currency

The base currency is the primary currency used to display all monetary values in your portfolio.

  • Default: USD (US Dollar)
  • Available Currencies: 22+ major world currencies
  • Symbol Support: Currency symbols are automatically formatted based on your selection

You can change the base currency at any time. Individual symbols can have their own currency set independently.
